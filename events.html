LWI Linguistics & Language Preservation Conference

Our flagship conference brings together youth researchers, field linguists, indigenous community representatives, and the public to discuss the urgent challenge of endangered language preservation — and what we can do about it.

Students × Scholar Forums

Our recurring forums connect high school students directly with field experts. Each session dives into a specific language, its morphology, phonetics, cultural context, and preservation status.
